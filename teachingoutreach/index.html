I believe that when people are supported and happy, great science follows. To that end, I have engaged in mentorship, community building, and teaching throughout my time at Stanford.


I am currently a co-facilitator for BioBuds at Stanford. I work with a team of graduate instructors to provide mentorship and training on the "hidden curriculum" of science and run a 10 week research internship for undergraduates. We also provide mentorship and teaching training to postdocs and graduate students.
