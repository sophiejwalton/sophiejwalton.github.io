I am broadly interested in the feedbacks between ecology and evolution in natural populations. My main study system is the human gut microbiome, which it is a highly complex ecosystem which exhibits rapid eco-evolutionary dynamics within human relevant time-scales. I have also assisted with research in ruminant microbiomes, soil microbes, fruit flies and yeast.
